Privacy Policy

Welcome to NIT Raipur Open Source Community (NOSC)!

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with opportunities and resources. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, and contact details when you sign up for our services or newsletters.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing regular updates on upcoming events, hackathons, internships, and more.
  • Hosting engaging webinars hosted by industry experts to offer guidance on trending skills and technologies.
  • Conducting frequent community meetings to address students' inquiries and concerns.
  • Offering opportunities to acquire new skills and bolster your resume.
  • Providing valuable insights on crafting a robust presence on LinkedIn and GitHub.

About Us

We are a community for the students, by the students, and of the students. Our goal is to provide resources and mentorship to help you excel in your journey. Your privacy is important to us, and we are dedicated to safeguarding your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
